Responsible Gambling

Sports betting involves financial risk. AI Football Predictor does not guarantee results, profits, winnings, or successful picks. Every prediction can be wrong, including high-confidence predictions.

Only gamble if it is legal where you live and you meet the minimum legal age requirement. Laws vary by country, state, province, and territory. You are responsible for understanding and following the rules that apply to you.

Never bet money you cannot afford to lose. Do not chase losses, borrow money to gamble, or treat betting as income. Set limits before betting and stop if gambling creates stress, debt, relationship problems, or loss of control.

If gambling is no longer fun or you feel unable to stop, seek help from a licensed support organization in your jurisdiction. Many regions offer self-exclusion tools, deposit limits, time limits, cooling-off periods, and confidential support.
