Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Summary for EU Visitors: In compliance with the GDPR and ePrivacy Directive, this site requests your explicit consent before deploying analytics tracking cookies. You can manage or revoke your selection at any time.

1. Introduction & Scope

AI Football Predictor publishes football metrics, match trends, and data-driven projections for informational and entertainment purposes. We are dedicated to transparent data practices. This policy details how we process technical information when you navigate our platform.

2. Data We Process Automatically

When tracking tools are authorized, we collect limited, non-identifying technical metadata. This includes your browser type, operating system, device parameters, approximate geographic region (inferred from IP address), traffic referral pathways, and on-page navigation patterns.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We leverage tracking cookies through third-party services like Google Analytics to evaluate site engagement and improve performance. These cookies drop small text configurations onto your device storage. You hold absolute authority to accept, decline, or systematically wipe these cookies via your web browser controls.

4. Form Communications

If you submit an inquiry via our contact modules, we capture your name, email address, and message payloads. This pipeline is used exclusively to evaluate factual corrections, manage affiliate integrations, address operational issues, or process compliance tickets.

5. Security and Infrastructure Boundaries

While our match statistics and historical calculations are accessed via a read-optimized public database client, all core administrative credentials, server API parameters, data ingestion endpoints, and structural tokens are completely compartmentalized and isolated from frontend client scripts.

6. Age Constraints & Gaming Disclaimers

We do not knowingly interface with or gather technical indicators from minors. All match projections and statistical references are designed exclusively for audiences operating within jurisdictions where viewing sports telemetry and prediction datasets is legally permissible.

7. Your Rights & Erasure Requests

Depending on your geographic location (such as the United Kingdom or European Union), you may possess legal rights to inspect, update, or completely purge your communication records from our systems. You can exercise these data rights by contacting us directly.
